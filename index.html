About me

As a UX Designer with an Engineering background, I have been advocating for users' needs for over a decade. I'm passionate about solving human-centric product problems and am engaged in accessibility and knowledge sharing.

My Design Journey

I started my career as a Software Engineer, working mainly with Android Development (Java, Kotlin) and web solutions (HTML, CSS, JS). Following my passion for solving users' problems, I migrated to UX and now work as a Product Designer and User Researcher.

I have worked in companies of all sizes throughout my career, including Samsung, Neemu, and Viaplay across various industries such as e-commerce, streaming, and information technology. As an Engineer, I can act as a translator between Designers and Developers.

I am also a chapter leader of Ladies that UX Stockholm, where we engage the UX community with relevant topics and encourage women and non-binary individuals to be speakers in a safe environment. My main goal is to help companies understand their users and develop meaningful solutions.
